# Quality Gates

Use this checklist for every photo before accepting it.

## Gate 1: Geometry And Crop

- The photo is upright.
- Horizontal and vertical lines feel flat, not phone-skewed.
- No desk/table/cloth/background outside the original photo remains.
- No album mat, picture frame, phone glare edge, white paper border, title/date strip, or scanner margin remains unless explicitly requested.
- People and important scene details are not cut off.

## Gate 2: Identity Preservation

- People count matches the original.
- Head positions, body poses, clothing, and age impression match the original.
- Eyes, mouth, nose, jawline, hairline, facial hair, and wrinkles are plausible from the source.
- In group photos, small faces are not individually beautified or replaced.

## Gate 3: Restoration Quality

- Dust, haze, scratches, fold lines, stains, and blur are reduced where possible.
- Texture remains photographic, not plastic or painted.
- B/W photos remain B/W; sepia remains sepia if the original is sepia; color remains color.
- Contrast improves readability without crushing dark clothing or blowing out faces.

## Gate 4: Packaging

- Final folder contains only finished images.
- Zip contains the same final images and no process files.
- Filenames are stable and ordered.
- The clearest approved source is what enters the zip.

## Recommended Status Labels

- `ai-accepted`: AI candidate passed all gates.
- `local-conservative`: AI candidate failed or was risky; final uses local faithful repair.
- `needs-human-review`: identity or crop cannot be verified confidently.
- `rejected-ai-redraw`: AI candidate changed identity, people count, or scene.
