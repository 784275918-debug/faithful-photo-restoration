# Quality Gates

Use these gates before a photo enters the final package.

## Gate 1: Crop And Geometry

- The photo is upright.
- It does not look like a phone shot.
- No desk, table, cloth, mat, frame, white edge, paper border, date strip, or title strip remains.
- No person or important scene content is cut off.

## Gate 2: Identity

- Same number of people.
- Same face identity and age impression.
- Same eye shape, nose, mouth, jawline, hairline, facial hair, and wrinkles where visible.
- Same body pose and clothing.
- In large groups, small faces remain naturally soft instead of being redrawn.

## Gate 3: Restoration

- Dust, scratches, stains, haze, and fold lines are reduced where safe.
- Texture remains photographic.
- No plastic skin or glamour retouching.
- Black-and-white stays black-and-white.
- Color stays color.

## Gate 4: Final Package

- The clearest approved source is used.
- The final folder contains only finished photos.
- The zip contains exactly the same finished photos.
- No private originals, candidates, rejected versions, or comparison files are included.

## Status Labels

- `ai-accepted`: AI candidate passed all gates.
- `local-conservative`: conservative baseline used because AI was unsafe.
- `rejected-ai-redraw`: AI changed identity or scene.
- `needs-human-review`: cannot verify identity confidently.
